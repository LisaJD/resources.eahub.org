# Forum
The <a target="_blank" href="">Effective Altruism Forum</a>.  contains in-depth posts, articles and questions on EA cause areas. You can get a nuanced understanding of specific cause areas, and see the latest research being done in the community by EA organisations and community members. 
# Conferences
If you’re able to travel, consider applying to attend an <a target="_blank" href="">EAGx or EA Global</a>.  conference, which are the main international conferences for the EA community.

# Newsletters

The <a target="_blank" href="">Effective Altruism Newsletter</a>.  is a joint project between a team of volunteers, the <a target="_blank" href="">Centre for Effective Altruism</a>., and <a target="_blank" href="">Rethink Charity</a>.  that provides news and updates about the effective altruism community. 

The <a target="_blank" href="">80,000 Hours Job Board Newsletter provides updates on the latest high-impact roles that 80,000 Hours has identified in the EA community and adjacent fields. 

The <a target="_blank" href="">Future Perfect</a> project on Vox takes an EA perspective on a large range of issues, including, but not limited to, main EA causes. You can sign up for their newsletter <a target="_blank" href="">here</a>. 

You can also sign up for the newsletters of the different <a target="_blank" href="">EA organisations</a> to stay up to date with their progress.

# Podcasts and Videos
The <a target="_blank" href="">80,000 Hours Podcast</a> features unusually in-depth conversations on the world’s most pressing problems and how you can use your career to solve them. 

<a target="_blank" href="">Rationally Speaking Podcast</a> Julia Galef speaks to guests as they critically explore a wide range of topics in philosophy, psychology, effective altruism, economics, technology, and more. 

<a target="_blank" href="">Future of Life Podcast</a> Ariel Conn from FLI in conversation with prominent researchers, policy experts, philosophers, and other influential thinkers of all kinds. Find a complete list of episodes above.

<a target="_blank" href="">Future Perfect Podcast</a> The Future Perfect podcast studies some of today’s issues with an effective altruist lens. The first season investigates a range of problems and interventions, and the second the pros and cons of big philanthropy. 

Watch talks from Effective Altruism conferences on the <a target="_blank" href="">Centre for Effective Altruism’s youtube channel</a>.
